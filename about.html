Creating Stuff, Probably Overthinking It.

I'm Anthony. A graphic artist, illustrator, and front-end web developer based in Atlanta, GA. My work is inspired by music, clothing, board sport culture (I don't really skate TBH), the world around me, and the random ideas pop into my head. I love creating things that can be funny, cute, and witty—often with a bit of a dark edge. From designing wearable art and conversation pieces to dreaming up entire brand identities (even fictional ones), I’m all about creating work that tells a story, starts a conversation, or just makes you do a double-take.
